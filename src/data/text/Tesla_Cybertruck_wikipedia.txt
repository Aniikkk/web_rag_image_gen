The Tesla Cybertruck is a battery electric pickup truck manufactured by Tesla, Inc. since 2023. Introduced as a concept vehicle in November 2019, its body design is reminiscent of low-polygon modeling, consisting of flat stainless steel sheet panels.
Tesla initially planned to produce the vehicle in late 2021, but after many delays, it entered production in November 2023 and was first delivered to customers later that month.
